What kind of information do you access from my Spotify account?
We prioritize your privacy and access only essential data from your Spotify account. This includes basic information such as your name, username, profile picture, playlists, library contents, and your top artists and content. Rest assured, you'll be informed of the data we access during your first login.
What platforms do you pull event data from?
At the moment, we source all event data exclusively from Jambase to ensure a reliable and rich database of events. As we evolve, we aim to integrate more platforms for a comprehensive and diverse range of event data.
